# Privacy Policy

**AI Memory OS**\
**Privacy Policy**

**Last updated:** July 2026

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Introduction

AI Memory OS is a personal AI memory system designed to help users
capture, organize, and retrieve information from their daily life.

Your privacy is a core principle of this project. We collect only the
information required to provide the service and never sell your personal
data.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Information We Collect

Depending on how you use AI Memory OS, the system may store:

-   Text messages
-   Voice messages
-   Photos
-   Documents
-   Links
-   AI-generated summaries
-   Metadata required for operation
-   Telegram User ID and basic profile information

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used only to:

-   Store your personal memories
-   Organize information into meaningful context
-   Build and maintain Life Events
-   Provide search and recall functionality
-   Improve AI-assisted organization of your own data
-   Operate and maintain the service

Your personal content is **never used for advertising**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## AI Processing

Some features may require processing by trusted AI providers, including:

-   Speech transcription
-   Text summarization
-   Classification
-   Semantic search
-   Context analysis

Only the information necessary to perform these functions is processed.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Data Storage

AI Memory OS stores user data using secure cloud infrastructure.

Depending on the deployment, this may include services such as:

-   Supabase (database)
-   Railway (application hosting)
-   OpenAI (AI processing, when enabled)

Data is stored only as long as necessary to provide the service.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share your personal information with third
parties.

Data may only be processed by trusted service providers that are
required to operate AI Memory OS.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Your Rights

You remain the owner of your data.

You may request:

-   deletion of your stored information;
-   correction of inaccurate information;
-   export of your data (future versions).

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures are used to protect
your information.

However, no Internet-based service can guarantee absolute security.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated as AI Memory OS evolves.

The latest version will always be available in the project repository.
